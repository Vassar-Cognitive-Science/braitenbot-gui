# Design Context

## Target Audience
Students in a class (likely cognitive science or related). They use this in a lab setting with access to Arduino-compatible robot hardware.

## Use Cases
- Visually design Braitenberg vehicle wiring diagrams by connecting sensors to motors through compute nodes
- Understand how signal pathways create emergent vehicle behavior
- Upload configurations to physical robots
- Trace signal flow through diagrams to reason about behavior before deploying

## Brand Personality / Tone
Evocative of cybernetics and the illustrative style from Valentino Braitenberg's *Vehicles: Experiments in Synthetic Psychology* — diagrammatic, scientific, slightly retro-academic. Think circuit-diagram clarity meets mid-century scientific illustration. Not flashy or gamified; intellectually inviting and precise.
